About Star Wars

       

Star Wars is a groundbreaking science fiction franchise created by George Lucas. It has become a cultural             Phenomenon, influencing pop culture and spawning a vast universe of movies, TV shows, books, and video             Games.

       

With iconic characters like Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, and Darth Vader, Star Wars continues to captivate             Audiences worldwide.
